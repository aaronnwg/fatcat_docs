# Getting Started

### Getting Started

Before you can trade, you need to set up your wallet and fund it with SOL.

FatCat Bot uses Privy embedded wallets. Instead of downloading a wallet app or browser extension, your Solana wallet is created automatically when you sign in with your email. The entire experience happens through Telegram.

Your private key is generated and secured entirely within Privy’s hardware-isolated environment. FatCat never has access to your key. Privy cannot access your key either. Only you, through your authenticated session, can authorize transactions.

### In this section

* [Setting Up Your Wallet](https://docs.fatcatbot.io/overview/getting-started/setting-up-your-wallet): create your wallet and understand how it is secured.
* [Funding Your Wallet](https://docs.fatcatbot.io/overview/getting-started/funding-your-wallet): deposit SOL so you can start trading.
